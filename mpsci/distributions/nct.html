Noncentral t distribution¶

Currently only the shape parameters (degrees of freedom df and noncentrality nc) are implemented.

mpsci.distributions.nct.mean(df, nc)¶

Mean of the noncentral t distribution.

mpsci.distributions.nct.pdf(x, df, nc)¶

Probability density function of the noncentral t distribution.

The infinite series is estimated with mpmath.nsum.
