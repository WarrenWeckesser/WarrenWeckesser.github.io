Rayleigh distribution

The PDF: \[f(x, \mu, \sigma) = \frac{1}{\sigma}\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}\right) \exp\left(-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x - \mu}{\sigma}\right)^{2}\right) \] The likelihood function for the vector \(x = x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_N \): \[ L(x, \mu, \sigma) = \prod_{i}^{N}\frac{1}{\sigma}\left(\frac{x_i-\mu}{\sigma}\right) \exp\left(-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x_i - \mu}{\sigma}\right)^{2}\right) \]
