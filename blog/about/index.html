About these notes...

This site contains a collection of notes on applied mathematics, statistics, signal processing and numerical computing. Some of the topics are elementary, while others are more advanced. Some are purely mathematical, but most will involve computer code. Some evolved from my answers to questions on stackoverflow, some are from working on the SciPy and NumPy libraries, and some spring from plain old curiosity about mathematics and science. My criterion for including a topic is simply that something caught my interest and led me to explore it further.

Questions? Comments? Corrections? Drop me a line.
